Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


This page provides help with the most common questions about LoopAffirm.


What are affirmations?

Affirmations are positive phrases or statements, typically used to combat negative or unhelpful thoughts but mostly to reinforce a positive mindset.


Is there a limit to the length of a recording?

Yes, a recording will automatically stop when it reaches 30 seconds.


Is there a limit to the number of recordings the app can store?

The app has a limit of 5 recordings per device. If this limit has been reached, you will need to delete at least one recording in order to record a new one.


What happens if I delete and reinstall the app?

Some or all of your recording data may be lost.


Do I need to allow microphone access on my device to use the app?

You must give your permission for the app to use your device’s microphone. Without granting permission, you cannot make recordings in the app. When you launch the app for the first time you will be greeted with a pop-up that asks for your permission to use the microphone. If you would later like to grant/deny the app access to your device’s microphone, go to device Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone and enable/disable it for LoopAffirm.


Where is my recording data stored?

Any recordings made in the app are only stored on your device.


Do you store any other data, other than recordings?

Aside from the audio recordings, no other data is stored on the device.


Is any of my data shared?

LoopAffirm does not share any of your personal data.
